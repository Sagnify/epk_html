Bhole Shankar

A Devotional Single by Kashmira Chakraborty & Parthdip Roy

About the Song

"Bhole Shankar" is a soulful devotional track that pays tribute to Lord Shiva. Written by Sujan Metia and composed and produced by Sagnify, the song blends heartfelt Hindi lyrics with powerful rhythms and modern arrangements. Featuring dynamic beats and captivating vocals by Kashmira Chakraborty, the song evokes energy, devotion, and spiritual reverence. Ideal for meditation, prayer gatherings, and festivals like Shivratri, "Bhole Shankar" delivers a profound and uplifting listening experience.
