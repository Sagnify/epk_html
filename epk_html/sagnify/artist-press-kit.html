About Sagnify

Sagnify is an independent artist, music composer, and producer known for his soulful compositions and introspective storytelling. His music blends contemporary indie vibes with traditional influences, creating an emotional and immersive listening experience. Handling every aspect of his music production from composition to arrangement and mixing, Sagnify works on a zero-budget model, proving that passion and dedication can overcome any limitations. His upcoming single, "Kya Kare," set to release on February 26, 2025, reflects his deep musicality and artistic vision. With an unyielding commitment to his craft, Sagnify continues to break boundaries, shaping his own unique sound and leaving a lasting impact on the independent music scene.
